12 years of sustained and sustainable development We seek that each step of growth is also a step in sustainability: that is why we develop our own technology to minimize the impact on the Patagonian landscape. We know that every innovation we make in production implies reducing water and energy consumption. Take advantage of raw materials All the bagasse of the malt that remains after cooking is still usable as feed for farm animals. In this way we are able to recycle most of the organic waste from the factory. Dose the energy Our factory works with an automation system developed in Bariloche. This allows us to precisely dose and regulate the energy we need. Minimize carbon To minimize carbon emissions, we use a single steam boiler for all cooking equipment. An integrated piping system also makes it possible to reuse a good part of that heat. Take care of water and life The lakes and water reserves of Patagonia are a priceless treasure. That is why purity is more than a starting point, our point of arrival. For a brewery to be eco-sustainable, the fundamental thing is to take care of our main ingredient. First, optimizing consumption. Second, cleaning the effluents by 98.5% through a pioneering treatment system in the Barilochense brewery. As a company with socio-environmental responsibility we believe that purity should not only be a starting point but also an end point.
